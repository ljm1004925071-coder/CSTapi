Field Monitor Object

Defines a new field monitor for the drift diffusion solver.

Methods

Reset

Resets all internal settings to their default values.

 

MonitorName (name  name)

Sets the name of the new field monitor.

 

FieldType (string  Type)

Defines the monitored 3D result field.

Type can have one of the following values:

Potential

The potential will be monitored.

”Electric field”

The electric field will be monitored.

”Electric displacement”

The electric displacement field will be monitored.

”Electron density”

The electron density will be monitored.

”Electron Current”

The electron current density will be monitored.

”Quasi-Fermi potential: electron”

The quasi-Fermi potential of the electrons will be monitored.

"Electron mobility"

The electron mobility will be monitored.

”Hole density”

The hole density will be monitored.

Hole Current”

The hole current density will be monitored.

"Quasi-Fermi potential: hole

The quasi-Fermi potential of the holes will be monitored.

"Hole Mobility"

The hole mobility will be monitored.

”Recombination & Generation”

All enabled volumetric and surface recombination & generation terms will be monitored.

”Plasma Frequency”

A monitor for the plasma frequency will be created.

”Total Charge Density”

A monitor for the total charge density will be created.

"Band Diagram"

A monitor of the band diagram will be created.

"Refraction Index"

A monitor of the refraction index  will be created. This option has the three following parameter.

 

Wavelength (double  wavelength)

Sets the wavelength of the optical simulation. This option is only valid for the "refraction index" field type.

 

VisualizeDelta (bool  activity)

Enables the visualization of the change of refraction and absorption index. This option is only valid for the "refraction index" field type.

 

VisualizePerturbed (bool  activity)

Enables the visualization of the perturbed refraction and absorption index. This option is only valid for the "refraction index" field type.

 

Create

Creates the source with the previously made settings.

 

Delete (name  name)

Deletes the specified field monitor.

 

Rename (name  oldname, name  newname)

Renames the specified field monitor.

Default Settings

MonitorName "Potential"

FieldType "Potential"

Example

With DriftDiffusionFieldMonitor

     .Reset

     .MonitorName "Potential"

     .FieldType "Potential"

     .Create

End With
